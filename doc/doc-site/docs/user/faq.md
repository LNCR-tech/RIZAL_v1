# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is Aura?

Aura is a student attendance management system that uses face recognition, geolocation, and manual operator fallback to track event attendance.

### Who can use Aura?

Students, SSG/SG/ORG event managers, campus administrators, school IT, and platform administrators can use Aura based on their assigned role.

## Attendance

### How do I check in to an event?

1. Go to the event location.
2. Use the kiosk or mobile app.
3. Look at the camera for the face scan.
4. Wait for confirmation.

### Why was my check-in rejected?

Common reasons:

- You are outside the event geofence.
- The event has not started yet.
- Face recognition failed and needs better lighting or a retry.
- You are already checked in.
- GPS accuracy is too low.

### Can I check in from home?

No. Most events require you to be physically present within the event geofence.

### What happens if face scan fails?

Ask an authorized event operator or admin for manual attendance entry. Manual entry should only be used as a fallback and may require a reason or note.

## Mobile app

### How do I install the mobile app?

See the [Mobile App Guide](./mobile-guide) for installation instructions.

### The app will not scan my face. What should I do?

- Ensure good lighting.
- Remove glasses or hats if needed.
- Hold your phone steady.
- Look directly at the camera.
- Ask an operator for manual entry if face scan still fails.

## Account

### How do I reset my password?

Contact your school's IT support or campus admin.

### Can I change my profile picture?

Yes, go to Profile > Edit > Upload Photo.

## Technical issues

### The app is slow or crashing.

1. Clear the app cache.
2. Update to the latest version.
3. Restart your device.
4. Contact IT support if the issue persists.

### I am not receiving notifications.

Check your device notification settings and ensure Aura has permission to send notifications.

## Event managers

### How do I create an event?

SSG, SG, and ORG officers can create events when their role has the required permission. See [Events Guide](./user-manual/events) for detailed instructions.

### Can I export attendance reports?

Yes, go to Reports > Select Event > Export as PDF or Excel when your role has report access.

## Administrators

### How do I add users?

Platform admins and campus admins can add users individually or through bulk import.

### How do I configure the system?

School IT and authorized administrators can access protected technical documentation for configuration, deployment, and troubleshooting details.
